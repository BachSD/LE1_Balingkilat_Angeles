__+__The Evening Nip__+__

Owned by a figure known as The Gentleman, the Evening Nip is one of many taverns in the city of Zadash. Located in the Interstead Sprawl, its shabby exterior masks a lavish underground bar unlocked to all who know a secret keyphrase. Per his request, due to the complications of the pandemic, an internet-based website was created for this noble establishment to bring the joy of well-brewed alcoholic beverages to the masses in these trying times. The Gentleman's pledge to give you exactly what you expect at the perfect price stands even through this pandemic. If you do not have coin, you can offer us... many gifts. Drink responsibly and enjoy!
